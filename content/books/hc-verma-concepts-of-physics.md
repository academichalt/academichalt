HC Verma's Concepts of Physics is widely considered the most important book for JEE Advanced Physics preparation. Written by Professor H.C. Verma of IIT Kanpur, this book is the definitive reference for building conceptual depth in Physics.

## Why HC Verma is Special

Unlike other textbooks that focus on formula application, HC Verma teaches you to *think* in Physics. The book develops intuition through carefully constructed examples and a progressive problem set that takes you from basic understanding to JEE Advanced level.

## Volume 1 — Mechanics, Waves & Thermodynamics

Covers the complete first-year physics curriculum including Kinematics, Newton's Laws, Rotational Mechanics, Gravitation, Fluid Mechanics, SHM, Waves, Geometrical and Wave Optics, Thermodynamics.

## Volume 2 — Electricity, Magnetism & Modern Physics

Covers Electrostatics, Current Electricity, Magnetic Effects, EMI, Alternating Current, Electromagnetic Waves, Modern Physics, Semiconductor Devices, and Nuclear Physics.

## How to Use This Book

Read the theory section of each chapter carefully, solve all worked examples without looking at the solution first, then attempt all exercises. IIT toppers recommend solving HC Verma at least 2-3 times.
