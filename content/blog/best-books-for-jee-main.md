The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is the most attempted engineering entrance exam in India...

## JEE Main Physics Books

- **NCERT Physics Class 11 & 12** (Mandatory)
- **DC Pandey Physics** — Arihant Series
- **HC Verma Concepts of Physics**

## JEE Main Chemistry Books

- **NCERT Chemistry** (Most important for Inorganic)
- **OP Tandon Organic Chemistry**
- **P. Bahadur Physical Chemistry**

## JEE Main Mathematics Books

- **Cengage Mathematics Series**
- **Arihant Mathematics by Amit M. Agarwal**
- **RD Sharma Class 11 & 12**
