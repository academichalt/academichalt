About Academic Halt

Our mission: making quality education accessible to every student in India — free of charge.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every student in India — regardless of their financial background or geographic location — with free access to the best study materials, NCERT books, and reference books needed for academic success.

👁️

Our Vision

A future where the quality of your education is not determined by your wallet. Where a student in a rural village has access to the same books as a student at a top coaching institute in a metro city.

Who We Are

Academic Halt is a free educational platform founded with a simple but powerful purpose: to break down the financial barriers that prevent talented students from accessing quality study material. We believe that every student who has the dedication to learn deserves the tools to succeed.

Our team consists of educators, former competitive exam aspirants, and education technology enthusiasts who have personally experienced the challenge of expensive study materials during their own preparation journeys. We created Academic Halt so that no student ever has to choose between buying books and buying food.

What We Offer

Academic Halt provides free PDF downloads of:

  • NCERT Textbooks — Complete sets for Class 9, 10, 11, and 12 across all subjects and streams
  • JEE Main Books — DC Pandey, Cengage Mathematics, OP Tandon Chemistry, and more
  • JEE Advanced Books — HC Verma, JD Lee, SL Loney, Irodov, and other advanced references
  • NEET Books — NCERT Biology with highlights, Trueman's Objective Biology, MTG Fingertips, DC Pandey Physics
  • UPSC Books — Laxmikanth Indian Polity, Spectrum Modern India, GC Leong Geography, and standard NCERT references
  • Study Guides and Blog Articles — Expert preparation tips, book reviews, and strategy guides written by experienced educators
