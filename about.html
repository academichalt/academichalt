Our Story

Academic Halt was born out of a simple observation: millions of students in India were spending thousands of rupees on study materials, coaching notes, and test series that should be freely available. Many aspirants from smaller towns and rural areas simply couldn't afford premium coaching institutes or expensive study materials.

We started by curating the best free study materials available online and organizing them in one easy-to-find place. What began as a small collection of UPSC notes has now grown into a comprehensive platform with 10,000+ resources covering 500+ examinations — from Class 6 to competitive exams like UPSC, JEE, NEET, GATE, and banking exams.

Today, Academic Halt serves over 2 million students every month, helping them access quality study materials without paying a single rupee. And we're just getting started.
