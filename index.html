About Academic Halt — Your Free Book Platform

Academic Halt is a free educational platform dedicated to providing high-quality PDF books, NCERT textbooks, and study materials to students across India. Whether you are preparing for your Class 9 annual exams, the Class 10 board exams, the competitive atmosphere of the Class 12 boards, or the high-stakes entrance exams like JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, and UPSC, Academic Halt has you covered — completely free of charge.

Why Students Choose Academic Halt

Education should never be a privilege. Academic Halt was founded on the belief that every student — regardless of their financial background or geographic location — deserves access to the same quality of study material as those attending the most prestigious coaching institutes. Our platform hosts PDF versions of popular reference books, NCERT textbooks, solved question papers, and more.

  • 100% Free: No subscription, no registration, no hidden charges. All books are available to download freely.
  • Curated for Each Exam: Our books are carefully selected and categorised by experienced educators to match what is actually tested in each exam.
  • Mobile Friendly: Read and download PDFs on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop.
  • Regularly Updated: New books and editions are added regularly to keep pace with syllabus changes.

Books for Class 9 & Class 10 Board Exams

The NCERT textbooks are the backbone of Class 9 and Class 10 preparation. Every student appearing for the CBSE board exams must have a thorough command of NCERT Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, and Hindi. Academic Halt hosts PDF versions of all NCERT books for Class 9 and Class 10 along with supplementary reference books that help students go beyond the basics.

Class 11 & Class 12 Study Materials

Class 11 and 12 are the most critical years of a student's academic journey. The foundation built in these two years determines both board exam performance and competitive exam outcomes. Academic Halt provides NCERT books for all streams — Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics), Commerce (Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies), and Arts (History, Political Science, Geography) for Class 11 and 12.

JEE Main & JEE Advanced Preparation Books

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is one of the most competitive engineering entrance exams in the world. To clear JEE Main and reach the coveted JEE Advanced stage, students need to go well beyond NCERT. Academic Halt provides free access to books like DC Pandey Physics, HC Verma Concepts of Physics, Cengage Mathematics, OP Tandon Chemistry, and many more — the same books used by top rankers at top coaching institutes.

NEET Books & Study Materials

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is the gateway to medical colleges in India. Biology contributes the most marks in NEET, and NCERT Biology Class 11 and 12 form the core of NEET preparation. Alongside NCERT, books like Trueman's Biology, MTG Fingertips, and DC Pandey Physics are essential for a complete NEET preparation strategy. Find all of these on Academic Halt, free to download.

UPSC IAS Preparation Books

The UPSC Civil Services Examination is the most prestigious and comprehensive exam in India. Aspirants need to cover a vast syllabus that spans Indian Polity, History, Geography, Economy, Science & Technology, Current Affairs, and more. Academic Halt provides free access to standard UPSC reference books like Laxmikanth's Indian Polity, Spectrum Modern India, GC Leong Geography, Ramesh Singh Economy, and NCERT books across all subjects.
