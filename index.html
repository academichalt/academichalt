Free Study Materials for All Competitive Exams in India

Academic Halt is India's most comprehensive free online education platform, providing high-quality study materials for students preparing for a wide range of competitive exams, board exams, and university courses. Whether you're preparing for UPSC, JEE, NEET, SSC, or your Class 10 and 12 board exams, we have the resources you need to succeed.

Why Free Study Materials Matter

Access to quality education should be a right, not a privilege. Millions of students across India struggle to afford coaching classes and expensive study materials. Academic Halt bridges this gap by providing absolutely free, expert-curated study resources that cover every major exam and subject.

Our platform hosts over 10,000 study resources including PDF notes, complete textbooks, lecture video links, previous year question papers, mock tests, and comprehensive study guides. All resources are organized by exam, subject, and class level for easy navigation.

UPSC / IAS Exam Study Materials

The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Civil Services Examination is one of India's most prestigious and competitive exams. Our UPSC section provides comprehensive notes covering all GS papers including History, Geography, Polity, Economics, Science & Technology, Environment, and Current Affairs. We offer curated notes from IAS toppers, NCERT summaries, and previous year question papers going back 15 years.

JEE Main and Advanced Study Resources

Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is the gateway to India's premier engineering institutes including IITs, NITs, and IIITs. Our JEE section includes complete formula sheets for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, topic-wise notes, solved examples, and previous year papers. Resources are aligned with the latest NTA syllabus and exam pattern.

NEET Biology, Physics & Chemistry Notes

National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is the single medical entrance exam for MBBS and BDS admissions. Our NEET resources include chapter-wise notes for all three subjects, NCERT-based summaries, high-yield fact sheets, and topic-wise MCQ banks. Special focus on Botany, Zoology, Organic Chemistry, and Modern Physics chapters that frequently appear in NEET.

SSC CGL, CHSL, MTS Study Materials

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) exams are among the most popular government job examinations in India. We provide comprehensive notes for Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, English Language, and General Awareness. Our SSC section includes Tier 1 and Tier 2 preparation materials, previous year papers, and sectional mock tests.

Class 10 & 12 CBSE Notes and NCERT Solutions

Board examination preparation is crucial for every student's academic journey. Academic Halt provides complete NCERT solutions for all subjects, chapter summaries, important questions, and previous year board papers for both Class 10 and Class 12. Subject coverage includes Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Economics, Accountancy, Business Studies, History, Geography, Political Science, and English.

Banking Exams - IBPS PO, SBI PO, RBI Grade B

Bank job examinations conducted by IBPS and SBI attract millions of applicants every year. Our banking section provides study materials for all banking exams including IBPS PO, IBPS Clerk, SBI PO, SBI Clerk, RBI Grade B, and NABARD. Special focus on Quantitative Aptitude shortcuts, English language preparation, and Banking Awareness.

GATE Study Materials for Engineering Students

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is essential for engineering graduates seeking M.Tech admissions or PSU jobs. We provide subject-wise notes for all GATE streams including Computer Science, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, and Electrical Engineering. Resources include topic-wise question banks and previous year solved papers.

State PCS Exams Study Materials

State Public Service Commission exams including UPPSC, BPSC, MPPSC, RPSC, WBPSC, and TNPSC require state-specific preparation. Our state PCS section covers state-specific GK, geography, culture, economy, and governance alongside standard general studies content.
