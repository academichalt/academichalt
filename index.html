About Academic Halt — Your Free Study Material Platform

Academic Halt is India's dedicated free book platform for students from Class 9 to Class 12 and competitive exam aspirants preparing for JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, and UPSC. Our mission is simple: every student deserves access to quality study materials regardless of their financial background. We believe that knowledge should be free, accessible, and well-organized.

Free Books for Class 9 and Class 10 Students

For school students, we offer the complete collection of NCERT textbooks for Class 9 and Class 10 covering subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, Hindi, and Sanskrit. These are the same books recommended by CBSE for board examinations. In addition to NCERT, we provide reference books and previous year question papers to help students score higher in their board exams.

Class 11 and Class 12 Study Materials

Class 11 and Class 12 are the most critical years of a student's academic life. Whether you are from the Science, Commerce, or Arts stream, Academic Halt provides free PDF books for all streams. For Science students, we have complete collections of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics textbooks including NCERT and popular reference books like H.C. Verma, R.D. Sharma, and Pradeep's.

JEE Main and JEE Advanced Preparation Books

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination) is one of the most competitive engineering entrance exams in India. Academic Halt provides free PDF books for JEE Main and JEE Advanced preparation including Physics by H.C. Verma, Mathematics by S.L. Loney, Chemistry by O.P. Tandon, and solved papers from previous years. These books are handpicked by top IIT rankers and coaching experts.

NEET Preparation — Free Biology, Physics & Chemistry Books

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) requires deep understanding of Biology, Physics, and Chemistry. Academic Halt provides complete NCERT Biology for Class 11 and 12 (the most important resource for NEET), alongside books like Trueman's Biology, DC Pandey Physics for NEET, and VK Jaiswal Inorganic Chemistry. All books are available as free PDF downloads.

UPSC Civil Services Preparation Materials

UPSC Civil Services is considered the toughest exam in India. The foundation of UPSC preparation is NCERT books from Class 6 to 12 in subjects like History, Geography, Polity, Economics, and Science. Academic Halt has compiled the complete NCERT series along with standard reference books like Laxmikanth's Indian Polity, Bipin Chandra's History of Modern India, and GC Leong's Physical Geography — all free to download.
