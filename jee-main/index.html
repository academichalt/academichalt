Download the best books used by JEE Main toppers — Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics — all free.

Best Books for JEE Main 2025 Preparation

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main is the gateway to National Institutes of Technology (NITs), IIITs, and other centrally funded technical institutions. It also serves as the qualifying exam for JEE Advanced, which determines IIT admissions. Choosing the right books is the most critical first step in JEE preparation.

JEE Main Physics Books

Start with NCERT Physics Class 11 and 12 — these are non-negotiable. Then move to DC Pandey's series which is widely regarded as the best book for JEE Main Physics. It has excellent theory, illustrated examples, and graded practice problems. H.C. Verma is better suited for concept building and can also be used for JEE Main.

JEE Main Chemistry Books

NCERT Chemistry must be read cover-to-cover multiple times. For Physical Chemistry, P. Bahadur is the standard reference. For Organic Chemistry, OP Tandon or Morrison Boyd are the top choices. For Inorganic Chemistry, NCERT itself is sufficient for JEE Main, though JD Lee can be referred for deeper understanding.

JEE Main Mathematics Books

Cengage Mathematics series is the most comprehensive resource for JEE Main Maths. Alternatively, Arihant's books by Amit M. Agarwal are also excellent. RD Sharma Class 11 and 12 are good for building the foundation before moving to harder JEE-level problems.

JEE Main Preparation Strategy

  • Complete NCERT first — 60% of JEE Main questions are NCERT-based
  • Solve previous year papers from the last 10 years
  • Take full mock tests under exam conditions
  • Focus on NCERT exemplar for tricky concept-based questions
