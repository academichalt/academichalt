Class 9 Books — Complete Guide

Class 9 is the year when students transition from middle school to secondary school. The concepts introduced in Class 9 form the foundation for Class 10 board exams and beyond. Academic Halt provides free PDF downloads of all major Class 9 books to help every student succeed.

NCERT Books for Class 9

NCERT (National Council of Educational Research and Training) textbooks are the primary resource for CBSE students. All Class 9 NCERT books are available on this platform for free download:

  • NCERT Mathematics Class 9: Number Systems, Polynomials, Coordinate Geometry, Euclid's Geometry, Lines and Angles, Triangles, Quadrilaterals, Circles, Heron's Formula, Surface Areas, Statistics, and Probability.
  • NCERT Science Class 9: Matter in Our Surroundings, Atoms and Molecules, Cell — The Fundamental Unit of Life, Motion, Force and Laws of Motion, Gravitation, Work and Energy, Sound, and Natural Resources.
  • NCERT Social Science Class 9: History (French Revolution, Russian Revolution, Nazism), Geography (India - Size and Location, Drainage, Climate), Political Science (Democracy), and Economics.
  • NCERT English Class 9: Beehive (prose and poetry) and Moments (supplementary reader).
  • NCERT Hindi Class 9: Kshitiz, Kritika, Sparsh, and Sanchayan textbooks.

Reference Books for Class 9

Along with NCERT textbooks, several reference books can help Class 9 students develop a deeper understanding and practice more problems:

  • RD Sharma Mathematics Class 9: The most popular reference book for Class 9 Maths with thousands of solved and unsolved problems.
  • S Chand Science Class 9: Excellent supplementary science book with detailed explanations and experiments.
  • Together With Social Science Class 9: Topicwise MCQs and short answer questions for thorough revision.

How to Study Effectively in Class 9

Class 9 marks a significant jump in academic difficulty. Here are some study strategies:

  • Complete all NCERT exercises before moving to reference books
  • Practice at least 20 Maths problems daily
  • Draw diagrams for Science chapters to aid memory
  • Read newspapers to connect Social Science with current events
  • Revise every chapter after 3 days, then again after 1 week
