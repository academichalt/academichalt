What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into intervals (typically 25 minutes), separated by short 5-minute breaks. After 4 focus sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break.

How to Use This Timer for Exam Prep:

  1. Choose a subject to study (e.g., Chemistry Chapter 5)
  2. Click Start and study with complete focus for 25 minutes
  3. When the timer rings, take a 5-minute break
  4. After 4 sessions, take a 15-minute long break
  5. Repeat until done with the day's study plan
