📘 Class 10 Books — Free PDF Download

Prepare for your Class 10 board exams with NCERT books and the best reference books — all available for free download.

Best Books for Class 10 Board Exam Preparation

Class 10 is one of the most important milestones in a student's academic life. The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) board exams test students on everything taught in Classes 9 and 10. Scoring well in Class 10 opens doors to the best streams in Class 11 and 12.

Must-Have Books for Class 10

NCERT textbooks are mandatory for all CBSE students, and most board questions are directly framed from them. For extra practice and deeper understanding, reference books like RD Sharma Maths, Lakhmir Singh Science, and Social Science guides are excellent choices.

Class 10 Mathematics

Real Numbers, Polynomials, Linear Equations, Quadratic Equations, Arithmetic Progressions, Triangles, Coordinate Geometry, Trigonometry, Circles, Areas Related to Circles, Surface Areas & Volumes, Statistics, and Probability form the complete syllabus. NCERT + RD Sharma covers everything.

Class 10 Science

Chemical Reactions and Equations, Acids Bases and Salts, Metals and Non-metals, Carbon Compounds, Life Processes, Control and Coordination, Reproduction, Heredity and Evolution, Light, Human Eye, Electricity, Magnetic Effects, and Sources of Energy.
