Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Academic Halt ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website https://www.academichalt.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Site.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you subscribe to our newsletter, contact us through the contact form, or interact with our website features. This may include your name, email address, and any messages you send to us.

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, referring pages, time spent on pages, and other standard server log information. This is used to improve website performance and understand user behavior.

Cookies

Academic Halt uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use cookies to remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and serve relevant content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some site features.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. We may also use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our website and services
  • To respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • To send newsletter updates (only if you have subscribed)
  • To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, provided that those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@academichalt.com.

Children's Privacy

Academic Halt is designed to be safe for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
