## About This Book

DC Pandey Electricity and Magnetism is a comprehensive guide for mastering electrostatics, current electricity, and magnetic effects. The book features well-structured theory followed by graded exercises from JEE Main to JEE Advanced level. Special emphasis is placed on circuit analysis, capacitors, and electromagnetic induction, which are heavily tested in JEE. With thousands of practice problems and detailed solutions, this is an essential resource for any serious JEE aspirant.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
