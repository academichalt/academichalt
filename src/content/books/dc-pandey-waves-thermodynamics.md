## About This Book

DC Pandey Waves and Thermodynamics comprehensively covers sound waves, string vibrations, thermodynamic processes, kinetic theory, and heat transfer. Well-known for its step-by-step approach to problem solving, this book bridges the gap between theoretical understanding and exam performance. The exercises include previous year JEE problems along with new problems at equivalent difficulty. Essential for Class 11 students and JEE aspirants targeting high scores in the thermal and wave physics sections.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
