JEE Advanced Previous Year Papers from 2013 to 2024 with detailed solutions is the most critical practice resource for IIT JEE aspirants. This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.
