## About This Book

CBSE Class 12 Sample Papers and Solved Previous Year Papers covering Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and Accountancy. This comprehensive collection includes CBSE sample papers released by the board alongside 10 years of solved papers. ## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.
