## About This Book

Morrison and Boyd Organic Chemistry is a classic university-level textbook that provides unmatched depth in organic chemistry fundamentals including reaction mechanisms, stereochemistry, and organic synthesis. The writing style is clear and engaging, building genuine understanding rather than rote memorization. While it covers more than needed for JEE alone, advanced JEE aspirants use it to develop deep conceptual clarity that sets them apart in JEE Advanced organic chemistry.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
