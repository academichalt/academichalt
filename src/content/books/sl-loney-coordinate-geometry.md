## About This Book

SL Loney Coordinate Geometry is one of the most respected books on the subject, covering straight lines, circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas with mathematical precision. Its problem sets are challenging and develop the analytical thinking needed for JEE Advanced. Students who work through Loney's coordinate geometry develop strong visualization and algebraic manipulation skills. It complements any standard JEE textbook and is highly recommended for advanced preparation.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
