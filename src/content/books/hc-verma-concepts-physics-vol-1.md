## About This Book

HC Verma's Concepts of Physics Volume 1 is the most trusted physics textbook for IIT JEE aspirants in India. Covering mechanics, wave motion, and thermodynamics, this book builds deep conceptual understanding through logical explanations and challenging problems. Each chapter ends with exercises that test both theory and problem-solving skills. It is recommended by top educators and has produced thousands of IIT rankers over decades. An essential book for Class 11 students and JEE Main and Advanced aspirants.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
