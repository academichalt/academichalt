## About This Book

MS Chauhan Advanced Problems in Organic Chemistry is considered the bible for JEE Advanced organic chemistry preparation. The book is structured around reaction types and mechanisms, with multi-step synthesis problems that mirror JEE Advanced difficulty. Level 1 and Level 2 exercises allow progressive practice. Its matchless problem variety and JEE-specific approach make it indispensable for serious JEE chemistry aspirants. Often called the Irodov of organic chemistry by students.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
