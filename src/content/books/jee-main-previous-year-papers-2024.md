## About This Book

This comprehensive collection of JEE Main Previous Year Papers from 2010 to 2024 provides the most authentic exam practice available. This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.
