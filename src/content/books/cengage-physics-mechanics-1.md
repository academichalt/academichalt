## About This Book

Cengage Physics Mechanics Part 1 by BM Sharma is a premium study resource used in top coaching institutes across India. It offers a brilliant mix of conceptual clarity and extensive practice with single-answer, multiple-answer, and integer-type problems mimicking JEE Advanced patterns. Each chapter features theory followed by solved examples and exercises at different levels. The book is especially praised for its logical approach and is a go-to reference for students preparing for JEE Advanced.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
