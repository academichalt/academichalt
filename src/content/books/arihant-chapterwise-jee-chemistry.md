## About This Book

Arihant Chapterwise Topicwise Solved Papers Chemistry for JEE is a must-have resource for all JEE aspirants. It compiles all previous year questions in chemistry chapter-wise with detailed solutions. Both JEE Main and JEE Advanced questions are included, making it a complete practice resource. This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
