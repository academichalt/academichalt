## About This Book

IE Irodov's Problems in General Physics is a legendary problem book used by physics olympiad students and JEE Advanced aspirants worldwide. With over 1900 carefully crafted problems spanning mechanics, thermodynamics, electricity, optics, and atomic physics, this book demands deep understanding and creative problem-solving. Not for beginners, it is best used after completing basic textbooks like HC Verma. Solving Irodov problems gives students a significant advantage in JEE Advanced.

## Who Should Read This Book

This book is recommended for students preparing for competitive entrance examinations including JEE Main, JEE Advanced, and NEET. It is suitable for Class 11 and Class 12 students as well as droppers who want to strengthen their preparation.

## How to Use This Book

Start with the theory sections to build a strong conceptual foundation. Progress through the solved examples before attempting the exercise problems. Revisit challenging problems regularly for best retention.
